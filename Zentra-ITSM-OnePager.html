What Zentra is

A unified IT service management platform that handles the full lifecycle — capture, triage, resolve, govern, learn — on one data model, one modern interface, with audit-grade traceability throughout.

  • Service Desk — multi-channel intake, SLA tracking, escalation, watchers, bulk & merge.
  • Incidents — sev grades, commander, bridge calls, presence, post-incident.
  • Service Requests — catalog, approvals, fulfillment.
  • Problems & Changes — root cause, CAB, conflict detection, rollback plans.
  • Assets & CMDB — hardware, software, SaaS, licenses, real CI relationships, renewals.
  • Automation — rules engine, AI classification, escalations, outbound webhooks.
  • Reports & Dashboards — Ops, SLA, Agent, Manager + custom views.
  • Governance — append-only audit log, 100+ scoped permissions, custom roles.
