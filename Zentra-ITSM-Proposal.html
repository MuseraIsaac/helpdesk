02 · Product overview

What Zentra does, and the problems it solves.

Zentra is a unified IT service management platform that handles every stage of the service lifecycle on the same record of truth. Where many organisations stitch together a ticketing tool, a CMDB, a change-board spreadsheet, an asset register, a knowledge wiki, and a manual approvals chain, Zentra brings all of these into one coherent operation — without forcing teams to compromise on depth.

The problems Zentra solves

  • Slow, inconsistent ticket handling — agents lose time switching tools, re-typing context, and chasing approvals.
  • Lack of visibility into SLA risk — breaches are noticed after the fact, not before.
  • Disconnected assets and operations — incidents reference systems no one can trace; changes touch infrastructure no one mapped.
  • Manual approval and routing — change boards, escalation matrices, and intake rules live in people's heads or in unreviewed spreadsheets.
  • Reporting that can't keep up — leadership reaches for monthly counts, not real-time operational signal.
  • Governance without evidence — audit trails are reconstructed in retrospect, not captured by the system.

Zentra is designed around these gaps. Every feature in the platform exists to either capture work faster, route it more intelligently, resolve it with less friction, or govern it with stronger evidence — and to make leadership's view of service quality something they can trust at a glance.

04 · Core features & modules

Everything an enterprise service operation needs, integrated.

Zentra ships with the full ITIL practice set out of the box. Every module is integrated against a common data model — so a change knows its CIs, an incident knows its problem, and a request knows its catalog item.

Module · Service Desk

Service Desk & Ticketing

A modern, keyboard-first ticketing experience with multi-channel intake (email, portal, agent UI, API), saved filtered views, bulk actions, ticket merging, watchers, internal notes, AI-generated summaries, customer history, and a full conversation timeline. Every ticket carries triage controls (priority, severity, impact, urgency), SLA deadlines, and escalation history.

Why it matters: agents spend their time resolving — not navigating, reformatting, or re-typing. The result is faster first response, higher first-touch resolution, and a quieter inbox by Friday.

Module · Incidents

Incident Management

A dedicated incident track with severity grades (sev1–sev4), assignable incident commander, real-time presence, video bridge integration (Zoom · Teams · Google Meet · Webex), structured post-incident updates, and a major-incident command room. Incidents link to problems, changes, assets, configuration items, and the source ticket — full context, one click away.

Why it matters: when something is on fire, coordination is the bottleneck — not effort. Zentra turns major incidents from chaotic phone trees into a controlled, recorded operation with clear ownership.

Module · Service requests

Service Request Management

A configurable service catalog with category groupings, visibility rules (public / internal), per-item approval requirements, and a complete request-to-fulfillment workflow including fulfillment tasks, approvals, and status history. Requests can be raised from the customer portal or by agents on behalf of users.

Why it matters: standard work — onboarding, access requests, software installs, equipment moves — becomes a documented, repeatable, auditable service rather than a thread of emails.

Module · Problems

Problem Management

Structured problem records with root-cause fields, workaround documentation, and explicit links to the recurring incidents and tickets they explain. Problem status moves from open to under-investigation to resolved with full audit history.

Why it matters: stop solving the same incident twenty times. Zentra connects symptoms to causes and gives engineering teams a real surface to invest in permanent fixes.

Module · Changes

Change Management

Standard, normal, and emergency change types with structured rollback plans, real Change Advisory Board (CAB) groups, multi-step approvals, schedule conflict detection against other planned changes, and a rollback-used flag for post-implementation review. Every change links to its affected CIs and to the assets they sit on.

Why it matters: change is the most common source of unplanned downtime. Zentra puts a governance perimeter around every change — without slowing the routine ones down.

Module · Assets & CMDB

Asset Management and CMDB

Hardware, software, configuration items, SaaS subscriptions, software licenses, and contracts — all with real configuration-item relationships, lifecycle states (received, deployed, in-use, retired, disposed), inventory locations, financial details, and renewal tracking. Includes a discovery-sync framework, CSV import, and renewal alerts.

Why it matters: incidents reference real systems, changes reference real CIs, procurement decisions reference real usage. The CMDB turns from a stale inventory into operational truth.

Module · Approvals

Approval Engine

A flexible multi-step approval engine with parallel and sequential modes, configurable minimum-approver quorums, expiration windows, escalation chains, and full ApprovalEvent audit history. Used by Changes, Service Requests, and any record that benefits from a structured sign-off.

Why it matters: approvals stop being chased over Slack and start being captured as governance evidence — with clear ownership and clear deadlines.

Module · Knowledge

Knowledge Base

A first-class knowledge base with categories, draft / in-review / approved workflow, public / internal visibility, version control, and a separate public search endpoint for customer-facing portals. Articles surface inside the agent console at the moment of work.

Why it matters: the second time a ticket is solved is faster than the first — but only if the answer is captured. Zentra makes that capture an explicit part of the workflow.

Module · Automation

Automation Platform

A no-code rule engine with structured triggers (ticket events, schedule, lifecycle transitions), AND/OR conditions over any ticket field or computed property, and a rich action catalog (assignment, status changes, notifications, tags, approvals, outbound webhooks, linked-record creation, custom-field updates, and more). Every run is recorded with applied / skipped / failed state per action for full traceability.

Why it matters: rules that previously lived in shift handovers are now captured, testable, and audited — and the team can change them without a vendor consultant.

Module · AI

AI and Intelligence

OpenAI-powered intake classification, AI auto-resolution for high-confidence tickets, reply polishing for agent drafts, AI-generated ticket summaries, and a copilot surface on the ticket detail page. AI usage is governed and auditable — never a black box.

Why it matters: AI handles the repetitive and the obvious so the team can focus on the work that genuinely needs human judgement.

Module · Reports

Reporting and Analytics

Thirty-plus curated reports across SLA compliance, agent leaderboards, ticket aging, first-contact resolution, channel mix, and trend analysis. Custom report views, scheduled exports, and Excel / CSV export are built in. Insight dashboards surface service-health, asset-health, and ticket-volume signal at a glance.

Why it matters: service quality becomes a metric you can defend to the board — not a feeling you have on Monday mornings.

Module · Dashboards

Operations Dashboards

Pre-built dashboards for Ops Command Center, Quality & SLA Monitor, Agent Performance, and Manager Summary — with a customizer that lets every leader build their own. Real-time updates over a live event stream mean numbers are current, not yesterday's.

Module · Notifications

Notifications

A unified template-driven notification system across email and in-app channels, with per-event templates, follower / watcher subscriptions, and configurable defaults for assignments, escalations, SLA breaches, renewals, and approvals.

Module · Routing

Teams and Intelligent Routing

Teams, queues, and policy-driven routing — round-robin, least-loaded, skill-based, and "smart" (combining workload, expertise, and SLA risk) — with per-team configuration and shift-aware availability.

Module · Customers

Customers, Organizations, and Entitlements

A CRM-grade view of every requester: customer entity, parent organisation, support tier, VIP flag, contract scope, and full history. Service entitlements drive SLA, priority caps, and routing scope per organisation.

Module · Customization

Admin, Customization, and Configuration

Custom ticket types, custom statuses, custom fields, a visual form builder, custom roles, and a one-hundred-plus permission catalog — all editable by admins at runtime, no engineering team required.

Module · Governance

Audit Log and Governance

Every privileged action — every assignment, every status change, every rule execution, every approval response — is captured in an append-only audit log with actor, target, before/after values, and timestamp. Exportable to JSON or CSV.

Module · Email intake

Email Integration

Inbound email via SendGrid Inbound Parse or IMAP, with full attachment handling, thread reconstruction by Message-ID, spam scoring, quarantine workflow, and intake routing rules. Outbound replies are threaded back to the original conversation.

Module · Customer portal

Customer Self-Service Portal

A polished portal where end-users can register, raise tickets and requests, track progress, exchange messages with agents, browse the public knowledge base, and view their own SLA status — keeping low-effort questions out of the agent inbox.

Module · Monitoring

System Monitoring

A built-in admin monitoring console that surfaces the health of the API replicas, database, job queue, scheduled background workers, and upstream providers — so operations teams can verify the platform itself is healthy before they pick up the next ticket.

07 · Asset & operational control

Knowing what you own — and what it depends on.

Most ITSM tools treat assets as an afterthought. Zentra treats them as the substrate of every operation. Hardware, software, SaaS subscriptions, licenses, and contracts are all first-class records, with real configuration-item relationships and lifecycle states.

What you get

  • Asset lifecycle visibility — pending receipt, received, deployed, in use, retired, returned, disposed. Every stage is captured; every transition is logged.
  • Real CMDB relationships — assets and configuration items link to each other and to the changes, incidents, problems, and requests that touch them.
  • Ownership, location, and financials — every asset carries its owner, location, cost, vendor, warranty, and renewal date.
  • Software license and SaaS subscription tracking — seat counts, expiry dates, renewal alerts, and over-utilisation warnings keep procurement honest and budgets clean.
  • Discovery sync — assets can be synced from external discovery connectors or imported by CSV, with validation and a re-runnable job framework.
  • Change conflict detection — when a change is planned against a CI, Zentra surfaces overlapping changes and dependent systems before approval.
Every change knows what it touches. Every incident knows what it depends on. Every renewal lands on the right calendar.
